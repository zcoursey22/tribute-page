Tom Petty

Runnin' Down A Dream

Here's a timeline of Tom Petty's life:

  • 1950 - Born in Gainesville, FL
  • 1961 - Meets Elvis Presley and becomes interested in rock and roll music
  • 1967 - Drops out of high school at the age of 17 to pursue music fulltime
  • 1976 - Tom Petty and the Heartbreakers is formed and they release their eponymous debut album
  • 1979 - The album Damn The Torpedoes goes platinum, selling nearly 2 million copies
  • 1985 - Tom Petty and the Heartbreakers participate in Live Aid and their sixth album, Southern Accents is released
  • 1988 - Forms supergroup the Traveling Wilburys with Bob Dylan, Roy Orbison, and Jeff Lynne and they release Traveling Wilburys Vol. 1
  • 1989 - His first solo album, Full Moon Fever is released
  • 1994 - Heartbreakers' song Mary Jane's Last Dance wins an MTV Moonman award for Best Male Video
  • 1999 - Receives a star on Hollywood's Walk of Fame
  • 2002 - Tom Petty and the Heartbreakers is inducted into the Rock & Roll Hall of Fame
  • 2007 - His memoir Runnin' Down a Dream is released
  • 2014 - The Heartbreakers' thirteenth and final album, Hypnotic Eye is released
  • 2017 - Dies at the age of 66 of an accidental drug overdose
